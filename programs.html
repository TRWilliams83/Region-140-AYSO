Programs and Divisions

Region 140 offers soccer programs for different age groups and skill levels. Each division focuses on age-appropriate development, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

Division Overview

Division Focus Typical Player Group
6U / 8U Basic skills, fun, movement, and confidence Young beginners
10U / 12U Passing, spacing, teamwork, and game understanding Developing players
14U / 16U Advanced teamwork, competition, leadership, and preparation Older youth players

Why Balanced Teams Matter

Balanced teams help create a fair and enjoyable season for everyone. Players have a better chance to grow when games are competitive and teams are built around development instead of winning at all costs.
