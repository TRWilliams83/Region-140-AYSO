Welcome to Region 140

AYSO Region 140 provides youth soccer programs for players in Carson City and the surrounding area. Our goal is to create a positive, safe, and fun environment where players can learn the game, build confidence, and grow as teammates.

Our Mission

Region 140 supports the core AYSO philosophies of everyone plays, balanced teams, open registration, positive coaching, good sportsmanship, and player development.

Who This Site Is For

This website is designed for parents, players, coaches, referees, and volunteers who need simple access to information about programs, registration, schedules, and ways to help.
